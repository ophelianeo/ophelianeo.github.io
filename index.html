Bio

Frida Kahlo was born on July 6, 1907 in Coyocoan, Mexico City, Mexico. She was s painter known for her many portraits, mainly self-portraits. Her father was a German jewish photographer.On July 13, she unexpectedly died from pneumonia.



Frida had polio as a child and at 18, she suffered a serious bus accident, which led to 35 operations.During her recovery, she taught her self how to paint. Many of her portraits reflect her physical ordeal. Frida's Paintings are often identified as surrealistic, although she denied the connection.
